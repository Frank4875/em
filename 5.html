WE GO TO GREAT LENGTHS TO MAKE EVERYONE'S STAY TRULY ENJOYABLE

We Make It Personal

Larchwood Care has care homes across the UK, which are either purpose-built or thoughtfully converted into industry-leading care homes. Our vibrant communities provide the perfect opportunity for you to enhance your quality of life and enjoy the way you wish to live.

Residential Care

Expert staff provide support on every level you may require, be it companionship to formal care. Such packages are led by a Senior Carer who will, with your involvement, devise a bespoke package that assesses all of your needs.

Nursing Care

Our nursing care packages are led by a qualified Nurse who, with your involvement, will develop your healthcare plans. They are supported by a team of care staff who will assist you to meet all your personal care needs.

Respite Care

You can stay for as long as you like, or for a little relaxation, reset and recovery. We offer flexible respite stays tailored to your individual needs.
